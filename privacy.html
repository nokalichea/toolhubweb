Privacy policy

Your privacy matters.

ToolHub is designed so many utilities process data directly inside your browser. Image compression, password generation, text formatting, hashing, calculators, and similar tools do not require you to create an account.

We do not sell personal information. We may collect limited, non-sensitive usage information such as page views, browser type, approximate region, and performance data to improve reliability and understand which tools are useful.

ToolHub may display advertisements in limited placements. Advertising partners, including Google AdSense if enabled, may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

Files and text you process should be reviewed before use. Avoid entering highly sensitive information into any online tool unless you understand how the tool works and accept the risk.
